# EcoPulse Product Documentation

## Product Vision
EcoPulse aims to empower individuals to take actionable steps toward environmental sustainability. By combining tracking, gamification, and community initiatives, EcoPulse transforms eco-friendly intentions into measurable impact.

## Core Features

### 1. Authentication & User Profiles
- **Sign Up / Sign In:** Users can authenticate using Google Sign-In or Email/Password.
- **Profile Data:** Tracks user's display name, email, and accumulated "Green Hours".

### 2. Dashboard
- **Daily Eco-Tip:** AI-generated (via Gemini) daily tips to inspire sustainable actions.
- **Quick Stats:** At-a-glance view of the user's Green Hours.

### 3. Initiatives (Impact)
- **Local & Global Challenges:** Users can browse and join environmental initiatives (e.g., Beach Cleanup, Tree Planting).
- **Status Tracking:** Initiatives have statuses (e.g., active, pending, completed).
- **Suggest a Challenge:** Users can propose new initiatives to the community.

### 4. Carbon Calculator
- **Footprint Estimation:** Users input their transport, energy usage, and diet to estimate their monthly carbon footprint.
- **AI Suggestions:** Receives personalized, AI-generated suggestions on how to reduce their specific footprint.

### 5. Portfolio
- **Impact Tracking:** Displays the number of initiatives joined and completed.
- **Recent Activity:** A timeline of the user's recent eco-friendly actions and joined challenges.

## User Roles

- **Standard User:** Can join initiatives, calculate carbon footprint, and track their portfolio.
- **Admin:** (Planned/Partial) Can approve suggested challenges and manage global initiatives. Currently, admin status is checked via specific email addresses for seeding initial data.

## Future Roadmap

1. **Admin Approval Workflow:** A dedicated UI for administrators to review, approve, or reject user-suggested challenges.
2. **Leaderboards & Gamification:** Global and local leaderboards ranking users by Green Hours, plus achievement badges.
3. **Social Sharing:** Enhanced ability to share impact cards and completed initiatives to social media platforms.
4. **Enhanced User Profiles:** Allow users to customize their avatars, bios, and privacy settings.
